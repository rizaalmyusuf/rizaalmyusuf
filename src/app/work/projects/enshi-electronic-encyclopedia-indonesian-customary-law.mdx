## Overview

Electronic encyclopedia on customary legal systems in Indonesia.

## Key Features

- **Dictionary**: Looking for the meaning of terms regarding the Indonesian customary legal system.
- **Maps**: Mapping the Indonesian customary law system by ethnic.
- **Definition**: Explaining the meaning of Indonesian customary legal system.
- **Quiz (Pretest-Posttest)**: Test for student.
- **Feedback**: Recommendation for project team.

## Technologies Used

- **CodeIgniter 4**: For fast server-rendered PHP applications.
- **MySQL**: For managing database applications.
- **Bootstrap**: For styling interface applications.
- **Quizziz**: For test the students.

## Outcome

This project passed the first funding stage in the social and humanities research student creativity week competition held by the Indonesian Ministry of Higher Education.
